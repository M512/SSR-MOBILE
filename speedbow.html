About Solar Speed Bow™

Solar SpeedBow™ is a Commercial and Utility scale solar system. Installations have never been faster or easier thanks to the all-new Self-Grounded Solar SpeedBow™ available only from Solar SpeedRack®. This universal and adjustable flat roof non-penetrating surface system is designed to fit all PV panel sizes with thicknesses between 30mm to 50mm, is adjustable between 0° and 30°, and requires little to no extra parts or accessories for installation.

  • Self-Grounded (UL467 Tested)
  • Faster installation times
  • Reduced installation costs
  • Eliminates the need to stock multiple rail sizes
  • Easy compatibility with all PV panel sizes
  • Requires fewer parts and materials
  • Rigorously tested to meet all structural integrity and wind standards
  • 25-year limited warranty
